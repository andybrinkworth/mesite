# Career Low — Presentation Script

---

Three years into my technology career, things were going well.

I'd made the move from hospitality, found my feet, and was starting to build something.

My best friend — my best man — needed a change. He was ready to move into tech and I believed in him. So I recommended him for a junior role in the company.

He got it. He was good at it.

Then the business made a decision to remove the entire team.

I had to make him redundant.

---

We had a couples skiing holiday booked for the following week.

Pre-planned. Months in advance.

We went anyway.

---

I won't pretend that wasn't one of the most uncomfortable weeks of my life.

But here's what I learned:

How you do something matters as much as what you do.

I couldn't change the decision. But I could be honest with him. I could be direct. I could make sure he knew it had nothing to do with his capability.

We are still best friends today.

And I have led differently ever since — because I know what it feels like to sit across from someone you care about and have to make a call that costs them.

That's what shapes you.

---

*[Pause — let it land]*

---

**Running time:** approx. 90 seconds at a natural pace.

**Tone notes:**
- Conversational, not rehearsed
- The pause before "We went anyway" is the moment — hold it
- The skiing detail gets a reaction — don't rush past it
- End quietly, not with a flourish
